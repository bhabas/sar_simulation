Failure Modes

1) NaN in model
    - CF_DC has NaN in state 
    - Solution: Restart Gazebo

2) Gazebo freezes
    - No clock messages
    - Solution: Restart Gazebo

3) Gazebo crashes and model breaks
    - Solution: Restart Gazebo

4) Can't communicate with CF_DC
    - Solution: Restart CF_DC

5) Can't communicate with Controller
    - Solution: Restart Controller

6) Gazebo stuck in reboot loop
    - Solution: Limit reboot loop
    - If limit exceeded then full simulation restart
